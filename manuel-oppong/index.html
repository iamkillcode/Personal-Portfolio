More about me

I hold a BSc in Chemistry from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), where I specialized in computational chemistry and catalysis. My final-year project investigated the decomposition of hydrous hydrazine on nickel catalysts, demonstrating my analytical and research capabilities.

Currently, I serve at the Food and Drugs Authority (FDA) in Accra, working with the Drug Physicochemical Laboratory and Supply Chain Management Unit. I manage vendor assessments, draft technical reports, and ensure compliance with public procurement protocols.

As an entrepreneur, I've launched innovative projects including ChaleCheck (a business review platform) and NextWave (a digital platform for underground artists), showcasing my ability to merge technical expertise with creative vision.
